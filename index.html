The Stigma around IBS

Unfortunately, IBS is yet another label which people use to discriminate against others and fence them into convenient areas of lower opportunity. If you are an IBS warrior, we beg of you to not be discouraged. We hope that this website and the following will give you some inspiration to fight on.
